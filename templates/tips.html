Helpful Tips to Quit Smoking

Quitting smoking can be tough, but these tips can help you stay on track:

  • Set a quit date and prepare yourself mentally.
  • Identify your smoking triggers and find ways to avoid them.
  • Replace smoking with healthier habits like walking or deep breathing.
  • Get support from friends, family, or quit-smoking groups.
  • Consider nicotine replacement therapies or medications.
  • Keep yourself busy to distract from cravings.
  • Celebrate your progress to stay motivated.
